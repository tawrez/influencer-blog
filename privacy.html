Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information.

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Welcome to InfluenceHub. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard information when you visit our website at tawrez.github.io/influencer-blog (and any future custom domain).

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or submit any personal information to read content on InfluenceHub. However, we may collect the following non-personal data automatically:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search term
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • General geographic location (country/city level, not precise location)

This data is collected via analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, if enabled) to help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve content and performance.

2. Cookies

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience. These may include:

  • Analytics cookies — to track page visits and traffic sources (Google Analytics)
  • Affiliate cookies — placed by third-party platforms (such as Amazon) when you click on a product link

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect some features of the site.

3. Affiliate Links

InfluenceHub participates in affiliate marketing programmes. This means some product links on our site are affiliate links. When you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at no additional cost to you.

We only recommend products we believe are genuinely useful. Our editorial opinions are not influenced by affiliate relationships.

4. Third-Party Websites

Our site contains links to external websites (such as Amazon, Flipkart, brand websites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before making any purchase or submitting personal information.

5. Google AdSense

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google’s Ad Settings.

6. Data Security

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as passwords, payment details or identification documents. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages which uses HTTPS encryption for all connections.

7. Children’s Privacy

InfluenceHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We recommend checking this page periodically.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@influencehub.in (active once custom domain is live).
